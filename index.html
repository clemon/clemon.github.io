Avionics Team Live data and video transmission

We’re working to launch one of the first student-built rockets into space. The avionics team works on the gathering of gyroscope, accelerometer, temperature, barometric pressure, and altitude data in addition to a live video feed. The data will be transmitted to the ground station in real time over ham radio. This data serves as the proof that we made it to the altitude we say we did.

We recently tested some live data collection with a high altitude balloon launch that led us into the middle of a desert to recover it. While it was kind of a pain to dig our cars out of the sand, it was definitely worth it, as the footage was amazing. Press coverage of our launch here:
